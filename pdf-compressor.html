Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes! All PDF compression happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your computer.

What's the maximum file size?

You can compress PDF files up to 50MB each. For larger files, consider splitting them first.

How does compression work?

The tool converts PDF pages to JPEG images with adjustable quality settings, then recreates a new PDF.
